About Us

Our mission is to make South Africa‚Äôs public healthcare system more transparent, accessible, and patient-friendly. Too often, patients travel long distances only to discover that the medicines or services they need are unavailable. This results in wasted time, unnecessary costs, and delayed treatment.

Our platform bridges this gap by providing real-time updates on the availability of medicines, hospital beds, and healthcare services across public facilities. By giving patients the ability to check availability before visiting, we aim to reduce overcrowding, improve patient satisfaction, and ensure timely access to care.

Our Goal

To empower patients with the information they need to make informed healthcare decisions while supporting healthcare workers and the Department of Health in building a more efficient, reliable, and trusted public healthcare system.
